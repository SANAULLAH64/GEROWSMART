I want to start growing tomatoes. What do I need to know?

Great choice! Tomatoes are rewarding plants to grow. Here are some key factors to consider:

  • Choose a sunny spot with 6-8 hours of direct sunlight
  • Use well-draining soil rich in organic matter
  • Plant after the last frost date in your region
  • Water consistently at the base of the plant
  • Consider staking or caging for support
